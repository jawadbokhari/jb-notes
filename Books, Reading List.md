## Learning as a crisis leadership
- *Leaders Eat Last* - Simon Sinek (if you haven't already)
- *Turn the Ship Around* - L. David Marquet (empowerment in crisis)
- *Dare to Lead* - Brené Brown (vulnerability in leadership)
- *The Culture Code* - Daniel Coyle (building high-performing cultures)
- *Multipliers* - Liz Wiseman (empowering others to do great things)
- *Start with Why* - Simon Sinek (for startup prep)

## For product strategy
- **"Inspired" by Marty Cagan** (product vision → strategy → execution)
- **"Escaping the Build Trap" by Melissa Perri** (outcome-driven product management)
- **"Good Strategy/Bad Strategy" by Richard Rumelt** (strategic thinking frameworks)
- **Product-Led Growth resources** (Wes Bush, OpenView Partners)
