This is about effective conversation and not just a command. 
Communicate with AI in ways that create a productive collaborative environment

Build **shared understanding** with AI instead of copying prompts from others and build
- shared vocabulary and references 
- well-defined goals
- values, processes, and methods

Three key components of Description:

- Product Description: Clearly defining what you want the AI to create - clearly defining what you want in terms of outputs, format, audience, and style
- Process Description: Guides how the AI approaches your request, which can be as important as specifying the end goal
- Performance Description: Define behavioral aspects like whether the AI should be concise or detailed, challenging or supportive
