The 4D Framework organises AI Fluency into four competencies. See the [[index|AI Fluency index]] for the overview and the individual notes for depth.

| Competency | Focus |
|---|---|
| [[Delegation]] | Decide what to do yourself, what to collaborate on, and what to fully hand off |

| [[Discernment]] | Evaluate AI outputs and processes critically, using what you learn to describe better |
| [[Diligence]] | Take responsibility for the work you produce with AI |

## Applying the 4Ds

Use this as a thinking prompt when starting any project with AI.

**Choose a project** — something you want to do in collaboration with AI. Then work through each D:

### [[Delegation]]
- What aspects of this project would you handle yourself vs. collaborate on with AI?
- Partner with AI to define your project vision and goals:
  - What is the overall vision? What does a good result look like?
  - What does success look like?
  - What are the different chunks of work needed to get there?
  - Which chunks require human expertise, creativity, or judgment?

### [[Description]]
- How would you communicate your vision — the tone, purpose, and success criteria — to the AI?
- Which of the 3Ps apply: Product, Process, or Performance Description?

### [[Discernment]]
- What criteria would help you evaluate whether the AI output meets your needs?
- Are you evaluating Product (output quality), Process (reasoning), or Performance (behavior)?

### [[Diligence]]
- What considerations around transparency and responsibility are important here?
- Who needs to know AI was involved, and to what degree?
